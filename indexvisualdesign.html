16-paged Magazine for a Non-profit Organisation    [Apr 2025]

This magazine was developed as part of an integrated communications plan for my chosen client in the Strategic Communication Design module (NM4208). In addition to the magazine, I also redesigned the official website for 365 Cancer Prevention Society, a healthcare-focused NGO. I deliberately selected a healthcare-related organisation because the cause resonated with me on a personal level. I found the process of curating educational content especially fulfilling, knowing it could help improve lives and encourage meaningful donations. Universe Infographic    [Oct 2023]

I designed an infographic about the universe, showcasing scientific data on planets, their distances from the sun, and other celestial objects like asteroids and comets. This was done in October 2023, as an assignment for the module 'Principles of Visual Communication'.
